  • 48 At this point, farming can be done at a location of choice, preferentially bringing Currency Drop bonus with Support. The general rule is as follows:
    -Farm one Bill type at a time (easier and more effective to stack single drop bonuses).
    -Aim for nodes that also drop a type of Point that is still needed.

    There are 9 nodes, with combinations of each currency and each point type, so a suitable farming area can be found for all cases.
    Check the Free Quests At a Glance table for a full list.

    Note: If you are shorter on Points than Currency, the three last nodes that open up after Part 1 (Mauna Loa, Mauna Kea, Kilauea Volcano) are 33% better, and are also better unless stacking single currency CE bonuses.
