Introduction

  • The Summer Servant Festival begins! Join Jeanne d'Arc (Berserker Alter) in her epic quest of overcoming deadlines.
  • There are three event currencies () and three event points () to farm for this event.
  • Main Quest progress is gated by total event points collected.
  • This Event follows a 7-day cycle with "Noon" / "Night" phases.
  • The general flow of this event is as follows:
    • Day 1:

    Noon    		 Main Quest if applicable

    Noon    		 Free Quest time (any 1x Quest)

    Night    		 Free Quest time (any 1x Quest)

    Night    		 Main Quest if applicable
    Day 2-6:
    Repeats as above.
    Day 7:
    This is a special Publishing day with no Free Quests. The time loop will start over from Day 1 after completing a Publishing Quest / Main Quests.
  • Every Main Quest has a specific Day and Time that it appears, some of which also require a certain number of total Event Points to be reached on that Day and Time for the quest to appear.
  • Free Quests must be done to force time to pass.
  • Note that Extra Quests (such as those awarding Command Codes, Costumes, etc) do not pass the time.
  • The below walkthroughs are split into two sections: The Main Quest walkthrough and the Free Quest walkthrough. This was done because Main Quests are largely static, while Free Quest and farming progress depends heavily on each Master's individual roster and how much Event Bonus they are able to reach using their Servants or CEs. The overarching recommendations will hold for all Masters, although the precise number of runs in the Free Quest section will depend on their lineups.

General Tips

  • Try to purchase the Event Shop CEs () first; the Free Quest walkthrough shows a strategy that tries to purchase CEs quickly while maximizing point gains.
  • When initially farming Free Quests, the lower-AP difficulty Free Quests can be skipped and left for later, since Main Quests will require enough points by a certain Day/Time to advance.
  • If the point requirement isn't met in time, then Free Quests must be repeated to advance time until it loops back to the deadline.
  • To avoid having to loop extra times before Main Quest completion, meeting point requirements ASAP is recommended.
  • Make sure to bring Event Bonus Servants! They will help increase drops of all types of Points. Bring as many bonus Servants as is comfortable for clearing quests.

Will I need to use Apples?

  • If just going for Max Ascension, NP5 Jeanne d'Arc (Berserker Alter), No. Otherwise, it depends on the event bonus Servants and CEs available, and how much of the shop is desired.
  • Natural Regen AP will be approximately 5700 AP throughout the event.
  • To only NP5/Max Ascend Jeanne d'Arc (Berserker Alter), this requires clearing the Main Quest line. Assuming no gacha usage, it will cost at least 4045 AP (depending on drop luck). Including Extra Quests for all Command Codes and Costumes, this will be an extra 40 AP, at 4085 AP Total. The AP cost can be improved with more Event Bonus Servants.
    • Assumes only 90% point bonus from Servants (no gacha Summer Servants, only Mash / Robin Hood, temporary Jeanne Berserker, and welfare Scathach (Assassin) + Ishtar (Rider))
    • No Gacha CEs (only CEs obtained free from the Event), aside from Supports, who do not need to have a Bonus Servant, and have an MLB 3/4* Gacha CE, or a non-MLB 5* Gacha CE.
  • For a full points and event shop clear on top of the above, with the same no-gacha conditions, an additional 4720 AP is required, for a total of 8805.
  • Clearing the Event Shop tends to be more limiting than clearing the Point ladder for most (who may have access to Summer Servants from prior years, Supports with Event Bonus, etc), unless a surplus of Gacha CEs are available. As a result, running with a support that has currency drop bonus is preferred over point bonus.
