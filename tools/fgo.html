Introduction

  • Welcome to the Fate/Accel Zero Order Lap 2 Event, a rerun of the event from two years prior! Many of the enemy waves in Free Quests have been slightly modified.
  • There are 100 Missions to complete in this event, accompanied with a whopping 50 Free Quests, and ALL must be completed at least once for full Mission completion.
  • This Event has a total of 7 Currencies, one for each basic Servant Class, each with their own Event Shop.
  • The Event Shop sells important items that are required for Main Quest progression--prioritizing those first will open up better Free Quests to help with Mission Completion.
  • The Event CE, His Rightful Place, can also be purchased from the Event Shop, although it has lower priority compared to the Main Quest progression items (at least when trying to go for a low AP / fastest clear). It should still be purchased as soon as the other Quest unlock items have been purchased.
  • The walkthrough below is an optimized clear totaling at 3345 AP for 100 Mission Clear (NP5 / Max Ascension Items for Irisviel).
  • Make sure to save EXP for Irisviel (to allow 2 Ascensions) -- Missions require her to be ascended twice for full completion. Also make sure to add her to the party as soon as ACT-18 is completed so she can start gaining bond points.

This walkthrough uses the below assumptions:

  • For Free Quests / Post ACT-EX Main Quests: Take Friend Support Servants with +3 bonus plus +3 CE [TOTAL +6] (aka Support Servant with a +3 or higher CE, such as His Rightful Place MLB / non MLB Volumen or Before Awakening) - or higher if possible.
    • The easiest Friend Support / CE combination to satisfy the above conditions for all quests is Anra Mainiiu + MLB His Rightful Place: + [MLB]
    • A full list of Bonus Servants and Bonus CEs can be found later on this page as well.
  • Master themselves (assuming first run) does not own any copies of Gacha CEs or His Rightful Place, and only gains CEs through progress in Event.
  • Master's Roster includes only Silver / Free Servants: Mash Kyrielight (with Self Geas Scroll, +1 Bonus to All), Kid Gilgamesh (+3 Saber), Diarmuid Lancer (+3 Archer), Gilles Saber (+3 Lancer), Hassan of the Cursed Arm/Hundred Personas (+6 Rider), Alexander (+3 Caster); Irisviel doesn't join until Seals are no longer a limiting factor in the run
  • Note:
    • can be equipped to anyone in a Quest that drops any currency.
    • - [CE on Classes] in the walkthrough refers to these CEs, which should be equipped on the listed classes for drop bonus in the corresponding step.
    • [Bonus Servant] - In each step, suggested Servants to bring that provide an Event drop bonus are also listed.

Should I Use Apples?

  • During the event period, approximately 4270 natural AP will be available.
  • A full 100 Mission Clear run (with NP5 + Max Ascension Materials for Irisviel, the Welfare Servant) will take 3356 AP. An additional full (complete) shop clear will take another approximately 1500 AP, depending on drop luck and Event Bonuses available, bringing the total AP to about 4856 AP for full Mission + Full Shop Clear.
  • A shop clear without pieces or monuments brings the AP requirement down from about 1500 to about 210 AP, making the total 3566AP for 100 Missions + Shop Clear (No Monuments/Pieces). This is very feasible with the natural AP during the event.
  • None of the Free Quests offer Ascension Material drop rates that exceed those of existing Free Quests either.
  • As a result, using Apples is not recommended, unless the Master is late in joining the Event.
