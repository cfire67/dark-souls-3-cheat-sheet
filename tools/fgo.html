Introduction

  • This is a 3-currency farming event ().
  • Free quests will happen in 4 rounds that are gated by race progress, which depends on Master participation.
  • In the rerun, an additional set of farming quests ("Maintenance" quests) will be available after the last round finishes.
  • The rerun also has a few quality of life changes, such as adjusting the materials and currencies dropped from each team support quest, and enemy compositions being changed from multi-class nodes into mono-class nodes.

Supporting Teams

  • Participate in a heated race between six teams! Race progress is determined by which teams Masters choose to support. Give your favorite team a boost by running quests with your team!
  • Special Boost Items will give a base of +2 points to the supported team, and will award QP at the end of the round for each boost used.
  • End of Round Awarded QP = [10,000 QP] x [Total Points Earned from Boost Items]
  • Get bonus points (+3 instead of +2) for using a boost preferred by the team you support! See the table below for a list of which teams benefit most from which boost items.
  • Use all boosts during the race! Unused Boosts can be exchanged for 10,000 QP at the end of the event. This means every unused boost is a loss of 10,000-20,000 QP.

  • The top 3 placing teams will have their associated Ascension Material rewards distributed to all Masters at the end of each round. A list of rewards for each team for each round can be found here.

General Tips:

  • Save apple usage for the post-Main Quest section. The best farming quests are the "Maintenance" quests, which open after the last round finishes.
  • Remember to stack currency drop bonus by bringing multiple Event Bonus Servants! They will increase drops for all event currencies.
  • Event Bonus CEs from both Part 1 and Part 2 of the event's original run will provide drop bonuses as well.
  • Buy Seaside Luxury ASAP from the Event Shop; this will help speed up the farming process for all currencies.
